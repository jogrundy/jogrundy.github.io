Conway's Game of Life

Modelling artificial life

Mathematicial John Conway developed this game back in 1970, here implemented as a cellular automaton with very simple rules

  • If there are three live neigbours, a dead cell comes alive
  • If there are 2 or three 'live' neighbouring cells, a living cell remains alive
  • If there are more than three live neighbours, a living cell dies
  • A live cell with less than 2 live neighbours dies.
