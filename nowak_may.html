and spatial chaos

M. A. Nowak and R. M. May, (1992) Nature 358 826-829 PDF

Each time step, every cell plays a modified 'prisoners dilemma' game with itself and its 8 neighbours. It then choses to mimic the stragegy of the highest scoring neighbour, if its score is higher than its own.
